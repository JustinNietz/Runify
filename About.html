About

Runify helps you plan your workout with music.

Instead of a boring stopwatch, time your run with a unique playlist designed by you!

Just tell this application how long you want to run and which songs you'd like to listen to.

After you press the Runify button, it will create a custom playlist that will last throughout the duration of your run.

Running while listening to music is relaxing. Don't stress about timing yourself since the music will do it for you!
